The Southeastern Solar Research Center (SSRC) was a photovoltaic (PV) testbed in Birmingham, Alabama, that was operated from 2014-2020.
This repository contains measured timeseries data from irradiance, weather, and power monitoring equipment throughout operation of the facility.
